// Operating Principles
01Roadmaps are communication tools, not contracts. The moment they become commitments, they stop being useful.
02The best process is the one your team actually uses — not the most sophisticated one you can design.
03AI doesn't replace operators. It multiplies the good ones and exposes the bad ones.
04Shipping is a strategy. Every week something doesn't ship is a week of signal you didn't collect.
05The gap between strategy and execution is almost always a coordination problem, not an intelligence problem.
06The operator who thinks like a founder is rarer than the founder who thinks like an operator.
